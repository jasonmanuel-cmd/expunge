Usage Rules
Strike Red — CTA only
Red appears exclusively on primary call-to-action buttons. Anywhere else it is diluted and loses psychological impact. One button, maximum contrast.
Never stretch the mark
Scale the logo uniformly. The icon and wordmark must maintain their proportional relationship at all times.
Minimum clear space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "E" in EXPUNGE on all four sides of the logo mark.
Cleared Green = outcomes
#27AE60 is reserved for resolved dispute states: Removed, Cleared, Confirmed. Never use it on CTAs or decorative elements.
No gradients or shadows
Flat surfaces only across all brand assets. Gradients undermine the legal authority and professional credibility the brand requires.
Approved backgrounds
Logo renders on: Sovereign Navy (#0D1B2E), Cloud White (#F4F6F9), or transparent. No other background colors.
